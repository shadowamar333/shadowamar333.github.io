ABOUT

Thrissur Motor Show (TMS) is South India's largest automotive exhibition,

held annually in Thrissur, Kerala.

Since 2000 , it has been showcasing the latest cars and innovations ,

featuring prestigious brands like Rolls-Royce and high-performance cars like

Formula racing cars.Today ,

TMS continues to evolve, with the 2024 edition themed

"Revving up the Future"

focusing on cutting-edge automotive technology,

including autonomous vehicles. Additional attractions include

the Engine Mortuary, Robowar arena, and Weapon Expo, drawing thousands of visitors each year.
